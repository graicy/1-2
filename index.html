Morning exercise


G. Landry, a fitness trainer and author of high-intensity weight loss programs, argues that one of the important elements in sports is morning exercises. Regular exercise helps to improve the general condition of the body. He identifies 10 reasons why you need to do morning exercises:

  • More than 90% of people doing exercises in the morning improve their results in sports.
  • Morning exercises contribute to a “jump” in metabolism.
  • When doing exercises, a person receives a charge of vigor and energy.
  • Many people say that morning exercises help them regulate their appetite throughout the day.
  • Morning exercise helps to awaken the body. Over time, the circadian rhythm adjusts to this mode, and a person feels better.
  • By practicing the exercises, we become more disciplined.
  • Studies have confirmed that physical activity stimulates mental activity.
  • By doing a 10-minute exercise in the morning, you can keep your body fit.
  • As a result of classes, physiological processes in the body will work better, which will lead to an overall improvement in well-being.
  • Just try to exercise in the morning, and you will see how great it is.

Jogging in the morning

Movie-driven jogging in the morning is widely accepted as the best alternative to exercise. What is more useful and better - exercise or morning run, let us decide for ourselves, there is enough material on the Internet on this topic. Undoubtedly, competent sports are useful in any manifestations, but not everyone will be able to readjust and start running in the morning, because it requires a lot of effort, willpower and time. In addition, there are discussions about when it is better to run, whether morning running is harmful. But if you are determined to run in the mornings, here are some facts and recommendations:

  • There is an opinion that the air is cleaner in the morning, which means that running at this time is more beneficial.
  • Thanks to running, the body is saturated with oxygen, muscles and joints are toned, metabolic processes take place more actively.
  • Those who run in the mornings are at times less likely to suffer from insomnia. The positive effect of running on the cardiovascular, nervous and respiratory systems is proven. During the run, almost all the muscles of the person are involved.
  • Running helps strengthen the immune system. Then, toxins accumulated in the body are also released.
  • Running is a serious burden, and an unprepared person can be injured. If there are any medical contraindications, it is best to consult a doctor first. Also always do a workout.
  • Of great importance is the route and equipment. You need to run where there are trees - in a forest plantation, a park. Clothing should be comfortable and not restrict movements. Shoes in size and soft soles.

A set of exercises for morning exercises

So, armed with confidence and determination, remembering the saying: “As you start the day, you will spend it”, you decided to do exercises in the mornings. There are many exercises, you can combine them and perform in any order. Below we will give a link to several ready-made complexes, but for now we will dwell on some important aspects that you should learn before starting the exercises.

  • Do not charge immediately after waking up. Get up, drink a glass of water, wash yourself, make a stretch, and after that get down to charging.
  • The body is not yet ready for heavy loads in the morning. Exercises should be smooth and not contain sudden movements. During class, try to breathe evenly and calmly.
  • It is also better not to resort to power loads. It is optimal in the morning to do aerobic exercises and exercises with your own weight.
  • Each exercise needs to be 8-10 times. Try to do exercises daily.
